Krzysztof Czarnecki

was born in Rypin, Poland in 1985. He received his M.Sc. degree in radiocommunications in 2009 from Gdansk University of Technology (GUT). He received his Ph.D. degree in 2013 in DSP from the same University. His research interests include the time-frequency analysis and sonar signal processing. Other interests are open-source programming and GNU Linux operating systems. Currently, he is an assistant professor in the Department of Marine Electronic Systems at GUT.
