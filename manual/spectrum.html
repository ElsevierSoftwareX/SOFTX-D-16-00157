Spectrum construction

1. tfr_fourier_spectrum(tfr_complex_signal* a_signal)

The constructor creates the object and calculates the Fourier spectrum of a signal which is the argument.

Arguments and return:
  • a_signal - transformed signal.

    2. tfr_signal_config* get_config()

    2. tfr_complex_signal* get_signal()
